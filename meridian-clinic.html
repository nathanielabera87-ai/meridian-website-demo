Our story

A clinic built on trust

Founded in 2008, Meridian Health Clinic began with a simple belief: every patient deserves unhurried, attentive care from a physician who knows their name. Over the past sixteen years, that belief has guided every decision we've made.

From our OHIP-covered consultations to our extended appointment times, we've built our practice around removing barriers to good healthcare — not adding them.
